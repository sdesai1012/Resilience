Your Story

I'm passionate about inspiring others to live purpose-driven lives. I grew up in India before my family moved to the U.S., and ever since, my journey has been about adapting to new environments and embracing growth. With over 12 years of experience as an auditor across the U.S., Ireland, and Sweden, I’ve come to understand the realities and demands of high-pressure environments.

A few years ago, I found myself physically and mentally out of shape—successful on paper but disconnected from who I really was. That discomfort sparked a personal transformation. I began the process of getting back in touch with my values and started building a life that truly aligns with them. My path has taken me across continents—from moving to new countries and pushing myself in endurance races to exploring mountains as a way to reconnect with myself.

Now, I’m in Thailand, immersing myself in Muay Thai training—a continuation of my journey of self-discovery through movement, discipline, and intentional living.
