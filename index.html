My Journey

I'm passionate about inspiring others to live purpose-driven lives. I grew up in India before my family moved to the U.S. when I was a teenager, and ever since, my journey has been about adapting to new environments and embracing growth.

With over 12 years of experience as an auditor across the U.S., Ireland, and Sweden, I’ve come to understand the realities and demands of high-pressure environments.

A decade ago, I found myself physically and mentally out of shape—disconnected from who I really was. That discomfort sparked a personal transformation. I began the process of getting back in touch with my values and started building a life that truly aligns with them. My path has taken me across continents—from moving to new countries and pushing myself in endurance races to exploring mountains as a way to reconnect with myself.

Now, I’m in Thailand, immersing myself in Muay Thai training—a continuation of my journey of self-discovery through movement, discipline, and intentional living.

Why the name Caramel Viking?

Caramel Viking represents the fusion of strength and sensitivity—a balance of fire and calm. The name was born unexpectedly while I was taking a cold plunge in Åre, Sweden, when someone shouted, “Caramel Viking!” and it stuck.

“Caramel” reflects my Indian roots, while “Viking” symbolizes the inner resilience I forged during my time in Sweden. It’s a unique blend of warmth and grit—about embracing life’s challenges with courage and determination while staying grounded in who you truly are.
